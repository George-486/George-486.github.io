The core gameplay of Genshin Impact is open world exploration, role-playing, and combat in the open fantasy world of Teyvat. Players play the role of "traveler" and travel through seven kingdoms, collecting resources, solving puzzles, fighting enemies through elemental reactions, and collecting powerful characters and weapons to advance the plot and complete quests. The game combines story-driven adventure, character development (leveling up, equipment, skills), puzzle solving, dungeon challenges, multiplayer online, and various activities.
