Role Introduce
Kamisato Ayaka Kamisato Ayaka is the eldest daughter of the Kamisato Clan in Inazuma, gentle and polite in nature.She is skilled in swordsmanship and uses the power of Cryo.
Raiden Shogun Raiden Shogun is the ruler of Inazuma, calm and dignified in appearance. She wields powerful Electro abilities and pursues the ideal of eternity.
Hu Tao Hu Tao is the head of the Wangsheng Funeral Parlor, lively and a bit eccentric. She masters the power of Pyro and is skilled in exorcisms and funeral rites.
