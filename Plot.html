Genshin Impact is an open-world adventure game set in a fictional continent called Teyvat, divided between seven nations ruled by elemental gods. Players take on the role of a traveler, searching for their separated relatives in Teyvat. Together with a guide named Paimon, they journey through these seven nations, exploring the world and uncovering its secrets.

Protagonist and Separation The Travelers are a pair of siblings from another world. While traveling in Teyvat, they encountered an unknown god who blocked their way and caused them to be separated
journey and goals Players will control one of the characters and embark on a journey with Paimon, with the goal of finding another lost relative and understanding the truth about this world.
Teyvat The continent of Teyvat is a vast world consisting of seven countries, each with its own unique culture and elemental powers, and is ruled by an elemental god.
Seven Kingdoms Exploration Players will follow the advancement of the plot, explore the various countries in the continent of Teyvat one by one, and understand their cultural background and myths.
Elemental Power The game world is full of elemental forces, and players need to use the elemental abilities of different characters to solve puzzles, fight, and interact with the environment.
Walking with Paimon Paimon is an important companion that travelers meet in Teyvat. She will provide guidance and help players solve mysteries.
