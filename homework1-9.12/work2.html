Bilibili

Bilibili, also known as "B Station," is a useful and engaging comprehensive video community, affectionately described by users as "an encyclopedic website, a library without walls, a refueling station on the road to growth, and a stage for creators."

As of the fourth quarter of 2024, B Station had an average of 103 million daily active users and 340 million monthly active users.

Centered around users, creators, and content, B Station has built an ecosystem that continuously produces high-quality content. China's best professional creators gather on B Station to create content across thousands of categories and niches, including lifestyle, gaming, fashion, knowledge, and music. B Station leads the way in popular culture and has become a unique presence on the Chinese internet.

On this foundation, B Station offers commercial products and services such as mobile games, live streaming, paid content, advertising, comics, and e-commerce, and has strategically expanded into cutting-edge fields such as e-sports and virtual idols.
