Mondstadt

Mondstadt is the main city of the Mondstadt area, protected by the wind god Barbatos, and known for freedom and calm.The city has nice views, busy streets, and holds many festivals that bring visitors from all over.

Inazuma

Inazuma is a region ruled by the Electro Archon, Raiden Shogun, and it stands for eternity and order.The region is known for its pretty cherry blossoms, old shrines, and many islands. The towns and villages have a clear Japanese-style feel, and the people there respect their customs and traditions.
